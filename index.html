Achievements

Tammy is a mother and a CPA. Among her achievements she lists raising her son and creating the successful business that she’s had. She started her business in 2000 and has been working for herself ever since, but she is approaching the end of her business and greatly looking forward to retirement. During her time working for herself, she has worked with many small businesses and their owners, handling both their tax work and monthly accounting. She has worked with many clients to help bring them success, all while also raising her son.

Looking Forward

Currently, Tammy’s biggest future goal is retirement. She hopes to retire soon, and when she does, she wants to spend more time in her garden and have fruit trees in abundance so she can bake and can her own produce to share. She also wants to have the time to help personally remodel her house with her husband, including landscaping work and interior do-it-yourself projects. She hopes to have the time for these and other projects once she retires. Ultimately, she wants to be someone people know they can come to for help and guidance and trust.
